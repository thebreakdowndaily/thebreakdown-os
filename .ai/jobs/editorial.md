# Job: editorial

Governing review — the Editor-in-Chief decides whether the chapter meets constitutional standards.

## Agent

`agents/editor-in-chief.md`

## Input

`artifacts/draft.md` (from narrative job).

## Artifact

`artifacts/review.md`

Contains: editorial decision (approved / approved with conditions / returned / rejected), written justification, conditions specified, revision brief if returned.

## Quality gate

Decision is in writing. Conditions are specific enough for the receiving agent to act without clarification. Rejected chapters include rationale for the Book of Record.

## State

`pending` → `running` → `complete` | `failed`
