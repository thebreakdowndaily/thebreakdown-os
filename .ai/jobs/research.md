# Job: research

Produces the research brief — the foundational evidence layer for every downstream job.

## Agent

`agents/research-director.md`

## Input

Editorial brief from Editor-in-Chief (topic, central question, scope, collection context).

## Artifact

`artifacts/research.md`

Contains: annotated source list, proposed claims mapped to sources, evidence gaps with priority, key entities/thinkers/timeline events identified, Six Questions with provisional answers.

## Quality gate

Research brief covers all Six Questions. Source list sufficient for minimum evidence targets (50+ claims, 120+ evidence items, 100+ sources). Evidence gaps documented, not hidden.

## State

`pending` → `running` → `complete` | `failed`
