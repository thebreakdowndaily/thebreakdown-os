# Job: architecture

Designs the narrative structure — the blueprint that governs the chapter's story.

## Agent

`agents/story-architect.md`

## Input

`artifacts/claims.yaml` + `artifacts/timeline.yaml` (from knowledge job).

## Artifact

`artifacts/story-blueprint.md`

Contains: central question, central conflict, story pattern, narrative arc with phases/turning points, evidence moments mapped to phases, decision points with alternatives, competing interpretations positioned, opening scene, inciting question, learning exit.

## Quality gate

Blueprint fits on one page. Story pattern declared and appropriate. Evidence moments mapped. Competing interpretations identified. Peer-reviewed.

## State

`pending` → `running` → `complete` | `failed`
