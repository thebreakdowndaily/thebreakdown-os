# Investigation Template

> **The question that cannot be answered by a single chapter.**

## Executive Summary

The central question, the scope of the investigation, the methods used, and the bottom line — in three paragraphs.

## Phase 1 — Define the Question

- What is known?
- What is disputed?
- What is unknown?
- What would constitute an answer?

## Phase 2 — Evidence Collection

- Sources consulted
- Sources not available (documented gaps)
- Methodological choices

## Phase 3 — Findings

- Claim group A (with evidence)
- Claim group B (with evidence)
- Competing interpretations

## Phase 4 — Implications

- What changes if these findings are accepted?
- What remains unsettled?

## Phase 5 — Methodology

- How evidence was collected and assessed
- Confidence levels explained
- Limitations disclosed
