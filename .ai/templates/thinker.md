# Thinker Template

> **A person whose ideas, decisions, or work shaped the subject.**

## Fields

```
Name:            [full name with diacritics where applicable]
Role:            [policy-maker | scholar | writer | diplomat | military | activist]
Lifespan:        [birth–death or active period]
Nationality:     [state affiliation at time of relevance]

Context:
- Where they sit in the intellectual or political landscape
- What drove their thinking

Key Ideas:
- Idea A (one-paragraph explanation)
- Idea B

Influence:
- How their ideas shaped events or scholarship

Appears in chapters:
- [chapter IDs or slugs]
```

## Rules

- Thinkers are not hagiographies or condemnations — they are explanatory
- A thinker's ideas are attributed to them, not to the school they belong to
- Competing assessments of the thinker's impact are documented
- Lifespan is optional for legendary or uncertain figures (marked with ~)
- The thinker appears in the Thinker Registry once and is referenced by canonical ID in all chapters
