# Timeline Template

> **A sequence of events with evidence, not a list of dates.**

## Event Entry

```
Date:     [precise date or range]
Title:    [one line]
Source:   [canonical source ID]
Claims:   [claim IDs this event supports or challenges]

Description:
- What happened (with evidence excerpt)
- What was at stake
- Who was involved
- How it connects to the chapter narrative
- Competing interpretations of this event
```

## Rules

- Every event links to at least one claim and one source
- Events without precise dates use ISO 8601 range (YYYY-MM-DD/YYYY-MM-DD)
- Disputed dates are documented with the points of disagreement
- The timeline is ordered by date, not by narrative importance
- Long descriptions (1–2 paragraphs) are preferred over single sentences
