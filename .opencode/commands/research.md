# THE BREAKDOWN
## Research Command v1.0 — Evidence Collection

### Mission
Gather every relevant primary source for a story. No second-hand citations.

### Source Types (Priority Order)
1. **Primary documents** — Court orders, legislation, PIB releases, government data, parliamentary records
2. **Official statements** — Ministry press conferences, regulatory filings, central bank data
3. **Independent verification** — Academic papers, WHO/WB/IMF data, NGO reports
4. **Expert opinion** — Domain experts with verifiable credentials
5. **News reporting** — Minimum 2 independent outlets must corroborate

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Supreme Court / High Court order (if legal angle)
- [ ] PIB release or ministry notification
- [ ] Government dataset (MOSPI, RBI, PPAC, etc.)
- [ ] Academic paper or institutional report
- [ ] At least 3 independent news sources
- [ ] International comparison data (if applicable)
- [ ] Historical baseline for trend claims

### Evidence Labeling
Every claim must be tagged with:
- Evidence type (Verified Fact, Official Statement, Independent Verification, Expert Opinion, Political Claim, Allegation, Unknown)
- Source name and date
- Confidence score (0-100)

### Gate
Minimum 10 primary sources with ≥80 confidence. Do not proceed to Verify without passing this threshold.
