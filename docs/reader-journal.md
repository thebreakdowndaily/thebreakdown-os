# Reader Journal

> **Never published.** Private observational record of real reading friction.
>
> Every entry captures: where I slowed down, reread, looked for a missing link, wanted more context, lost trust, or felt something click.
>
> **Target:** 50–100 observations before extracting patterns or solutions.

## How to Use

1. Open the Founding Monograph (Chapter 1) on desktop or mobile.
2. Read naturally — do not task-switch or inspect.
3. After each session, write down every moment of friction or delight.
4. **Do not propose fixes.** Just observe.
5. Once patterns emerge across entries, the strongest repeated friction becomes the next sprint.
