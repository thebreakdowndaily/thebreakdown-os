| **Evidence Level** | B |
| **IIIF URL** | https://www.npg.org.uk/collections/search/portrait/mw107297/Cyril-John-Radcliffe-1st-Viscount-Radcliffe |

### Rights Assessment (Updated 2026-07-14)

**NPG Licensing Model (post-2023)** — NPG now offers three licensing tiers:

1. **Creative Commons (free)** — For non-commercial online uses: scholarly websites, non-profit blogs, local society newsletters, family history projects. Free low-resolution download available.
2. **Non-Commercial Licence (free)** — For non-commercial research, teaching, dissertations, free-to-access publications under 1,000 copies. Free of charge once registered.
3. **Commercial Licence (paid)** — For any use not covered above.

**The Breakdown qualifies for the Non-Commercial Licence or CC option** — as a non-profit, evidence-based knowledge platform with no paywall.

**Note:** The asset register lists NPG x88321 (1957) but research found NPG x91143 (1949, Elliott & Fry) as a more readily available portrait. Any Elliott & Fry Radcliffe portrait should be functionally equivalent for the "authorised substitute" role.

### Verdict

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Can we publish? | ✅ Yes — via NPG Non-Commercial Licence (free) |
| Is the file available for free download? | ✅ Yes — register at npgimages.com, select Non-Commercial Licence |
| Does the required credit line change? | No — "© National Portrait Gallery, London" confirmed |
| Should we use x88321 (1957) or x91143 (1949)? | Either is acceptable; x91143 may be easier to obtain |

### Recommended Action

1. Register at https://www.npg.org.uk/collections/search/use-this-image/ for NPG x88321 or x91143
2. Select Non-Commercial Licence (free) and download
3. Place file and update URL in block data
